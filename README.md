# 📡 Megaline Telecom: Customer Behavior and Revenue Analysis

## 📘 Project Overview

This project provides an in-depth analysis of customer data for **Megaline**, a telecommunications operator. The goal is to compare two prepaid plans — **Surf** and **Ultimate** — to determine which one is more profitable and to understand customer consumption behavior.
The analysis is based on a dataset from **500 customers**, covering calls, SMS, and data usage across 2018.

The findings support the commercial team in making **data-driven decisions** about advertising investment and strategic direction.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives

1. **🧹 Data Preparation:**
   Clean, preprocess, and aggregate raw data, correct data types, handle missing values, and calculate users' monthly usage metrics.

2. **📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):**
   Generate descriptive statistics and visualizations to understand usage patterns for each plan.

3. **💵 Revenue Calculation:**
   Compute monthly revenue per user based on plan limits, overage rates, and base fees.

4. **🧪 Hypothesis Testing:**
   Validate two business hypotheses:

   * Revenue differs between **Ultimate** and **Surf** plan users.
   * Revenue differs between **NY–NJ** customers and other regions.

5. **📈 Actionable Recommendations:**
   Provide strategic insights to support commercial decision-making.

---

## 📝 Summary of Findings

* **💰 Ultimate Plan Is More Profitable:**
  Statistical tests confirm that **Ultimate users generate significantly higher average monthly revenue**.

* **📶 Data Causes Most Overage Charges:**
  Surf users frequently exceed their **15 GB data allowance**, leading to additional charges.

* **🛡️ Ultimate = Peace of Mind:**
  Ultimate plan customers rarely exceed their generous limits, making it ideal for high-usage users.

* **🌎 No Regional Revenue Difference:**
  No significant revenue difference between NY–NJ customers and those from other regions.

---

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations

1. **📢 Promote the Ultimate Plan:**
   Increase advertising efforts showcasing its predictability and high value for heavy users.

2. **📈 Upsell High-Usage Surf Customers:**
   Identify Surf users with frequent overage fees and target them with upgrade campaigns.

3. **📱 Create a Mid-Tier Plan:**
   Consider introducing a **“Surf Plus”** plan (25–30 GB) to capture a high-usage segment not ready for the Ultimate plan.
